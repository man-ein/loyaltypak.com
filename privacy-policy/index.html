Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

At loyaltypak.com, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services, including any SMS/text messaging programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (when you opt-in to SMS), or any other information you voluntarily provide through forms or inquiries.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze traffic.

2. SMS / Text Message Marketing & Notifications

When you opt-in to receive SMS messages from loyaltypak.com (for order updates, shipping notifications, promotions, or other communications), you agree to receive recurring marketing and transactional text messages (including via automated technology) to the mobile number you provided.

Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply.

You can cancel at any time. Text STOP to the short code or number that sent you the message. You’ll receive one final confirmation message, and you will no longer receive SMS from us.

To re-join, simply opt-in again through our website or contact form.

For help, text HELP or email us at the contact information below.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Supported carriers include major U.S. networks (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, Boost, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and others). Compatibility may vary.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide custom packaging quotes, order processing, and shipping updates
  • Send promotional or transactional SMS messages (only if you opt-in)
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage and improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., SMS platforms, shipping carriers, or payment processors) who assist in operating our services
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of SMS messages at any time (see section 2)
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our SMS program, please contact us via our contact form.
